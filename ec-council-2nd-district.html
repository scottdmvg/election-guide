Ori Feibush is a City Council candidate for the 2nd District. Feibush graduated from Temple University, receiving degrees in economics, risk management, and actuarial science. Following graduation he moved to Point Breeze, where he currently resides. Feibush founded OCF Realty in 2008, focusing on development projects in Point Breeze. In addition to his real estate development projects, he opened three OCF Coffee Houses in Philadelphia. Along with his business ventures, Feibush has worked on several neighborhood improvement programs and founded the Point Breeze Community Development Corporation.
